           KIRITO OPTIMIZER - ESPORTS TOUCH & SIGNAL OPTIMIZER
                       COMPLETE USER MANUAL & GUIDE
================================================================================

Welcome to the Kirito Optimizer! This step-by-step manual will guide you from 
uncalibrated, laggy gameplay to perfect, ultra-responsive sensitivity and 
ideal "Brazilian Sensi" ratios (perfect for Battle Royale and shooter games).

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
STEP 1: PRE-BENCHMARK (CHECK YOUR BASELINE LATENCY)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Before making any changes, establish your current performance baseline:
1. Scroll down to the "REAL-TIME TOUCH INTERFACE SAMPLING INSTRUMENT" section.
2. Tap, hold, or drag your finger rapidly inside the green benchmark area.
3. Observe your average/peak Touch Sampling Rate (Hz) and Jitter (ms). 
   - Write down or remember these baseline numbers.
4. Go to the "NEURAL REACTION RESPONSE TESTER" section.
5. Tap to initiate, wait for the screen to turn GREEN, and tap as fast as you 
   can to benchmark your raw system-to-screen processing speed in milliseconds.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
STEP 2: RUN THE GEMINI AI DEVICE ANALYZER
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
To get a customized tuning profile for your exact hardware:
1. Go to the "AI HARDWARE TUNER & SIGNAL OPTIMIZER" control panel at the top.
2. Select your gaming platform:
   - Android (Phones/Tablets)
   - iOS (iPhones/iPads)
   - PC (Windows gaming systems)
   - Console (PlayStation/Xbox/Nintendo)
3. Type in your exact Device Model (e.g., "ASUS ROG Phone 8 Pro", "iPhone 16 Pro Max", "iPad Air M2").
4. Select your current perceived lag level (e.g., "High Jitter", "Micro-Stuttering", "Low-Latency Default").
5. (Optional) Provide additional details like the specific game you play (e.g., "Free Fire", "PUBG Mobile", "Valorant").
6. Click "OPTIMIZE DEVICE" to query the AI Engine.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
STEP 3: APPLY HARDWARE & DISPLAY SETTINGS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Once the AI generates your report, look at the "HARDWARE CALIBRATION & DISPLAY" panel:
1. Read each recommended system tweak.
2. Go to your device's System Settings and apply them.
   - For Android: This often includes locking your display's refresh rate to the maximum (e.g., 120Hz/165Hz) instead of Auto, or disabling gesture shortcuts (like 3-finger screenshot) which buffer touch inputs.
   - For iOS: This includes turning off "Limit Frame Rate" under Accessibility > Motion, and setting Haptic Touch to "Fast".
   - For PC: Setting NVIDIA Reflex to "Ultra", disabling Fullscreen Optimizations, or locking mouse poll rate to 1000Hz+.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
STEP 4: SETUP THE BRAZILIAN SENSI & SYSTEM SLIDERS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Scroll down to "BRAZILIAN COMPETITIVE SENSI SETTINGS" to find your exact sensitivity numbers.
1. Configure your system-level Pointer Speed (Velocidade do Ponteiro) as specified.
2. Change your device's DPI / Minimum Width (Menor Largura):
   - For Android: Enable "Developer Options", locate "Minimum Width" (or "Smallest Width"), and change it to the suggested DPI value.
   - For iOS/PC: Set tracking speed scale as recommended.
3. Configure your in-game sensitivity sliders exactly to the generated values:
   - Geral (General Camera): Main sensitivity for swift target tracking and 180 turns.
   - Red Dot (Ponto Vermelho): Crucial for close-mid range tracking.
   - Mira 2x (2x Scope): Smooth target tracking.
   - Mira 4x (4x Scope): Calibrated drag speed.
   - AWM (Sniper Scope): Precise tracking without overflicking.
   - Olhadinha (Free Look): Balanced ambient control.
4. Read the "Capacidade Capa" (Headshot Alignment Engine) text to understand the swipe velocity and angle recommended for your weapon style (e.g., drag-shots with shotgun M1014 or SMG tracking).

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
STEP 5: INJECT SYSTEM-LEVEL TWEAKS (ADB / REGISTRY)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Locate the "OS REGISTER / SHELL COMMAND TERMINAL OVERRIDES" box:
1. Copy the commands generated for your platform.
2. Apply them:
   - For Android: Connect your phone to a PC, open an ADB command window, and execute the shell settings overrides.
   - For PC (Windows): Open `regedit` (Registry Editor) and navigate to the specified paths to set the mouse/network parameters.
   - For iOS: Apply the accessibility configuration rules (like disabling "Touch Accommodations" and adjusting repeat rates).

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
STEP 6: RE-BENCHMARK & VERIFY IMPROVEMENTS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Return to the "REAL-TIME TOUCH INTERFACE SAMPLING INSTRUMENT" and run the test again.
2. Verify that:
   - Touch Sampling Rate (Hz) is higher and more stable.
   - Touch Jitter (ms) has decreased toward zero.
3. Run the reaction test again. You should see a noticeable drop in reaction times because of reduced display input queuing.
4. Save your tuned profile to the "OPTIMIZED ESPORTS PLAYER REGISTRY" sidebar to keep track of your successful configurations!

Enjoy your lag-free gaming!
